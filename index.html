WHY WE HAVE CREATED THIS WEBSITE

This site is basically to take care of pet as well as stray animals. Main motive is to provide them food and take care of their health if they are suffering from any disease. During this pandemic, it is difficult to rush to animal clinic for the treatment so this site provides the better treatment at home with just one click.

About Us
